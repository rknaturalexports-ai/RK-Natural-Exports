SFD – SUPER FINE DUST

SFD represents the finest particle size in the CTC dust category, producing an intensely dark, full-bodied liquor with exceptional brewing speed. Its concentrated surface area releases color and strength within seconds, making it the preferred choice for high-volume tea bag manufacturing. Consistently delivers a deep, coppery-red cup with a pronounced brisk finish valued in South Asian and Middle Eastern markets.

Cup Profile

LiquorDeep copper-red, highly bright
StrengthVery strong
AromaMalty, brisk, slightly earthy
InfusionInstant extraction — fully brewed in under 60 seconds
Common UseTea bags, CTC blend base, fast-brew commercial teas

SRD – SUPER RED DUST

SRD sits just above SFD in particle size, retaining the rapid infusion characteristic of fine dust while producing a distinctly bright, reddish liquor with a bold, brisk character. It is widely sourced for domestic and export blends requiring strength without excessive astringency. Particularly in demand across India, Pakistan, and the CIS region for everyday strong-cup consumption.

Cup Profile

LiquorBright red, clean and clear
StrengthStrong
AromaBrisk, malty, lively
InfusionQuick extraction with high color yield
Common UseDomestic dust blends, CTC tea bags, strong-cup retail

RD – RED DUST

RD is a coarser dust grade positioned between SRD and PD, offering a well-balanced combination of color, body, and briskness. Its slightly larger particle size results in a moderately paced extraction that produces a vibrant reddish liquor with medium to strong strength. Widely preferred in South Indian retail markets and blended packaged teas where controlled strength is essential.

Cup Profile

LiquorBright reddish-amber, clean
StrengthMedium to strong
AromaMalty, moderately brisk
InfusionSteady extraction, good color release
Common UseSouth Indian packaged teas, everyday loose leaf blends

PD – PEKOE DUST

Pekoe Dust is the largest particle in the dust classification, bridging the gap between dust and fannings grades. It produces a well-rounded cup with balanced body and good briskness without the intensity of finer dusts. Valued by blenders who require dust-grade speed of infusion with a softer, more approachable strength profile suited for premium packaged teas.

Cup Profile

LiquorAmber-red, bright and clear
StrengthMedium
AromaSoft malt, gentle briskness
InfusionModerate extraction, smooth and consistent
Common UsePremium dust blends, mid-range tea bags, HoReCa teas

PF – PEKOE FANNINGS

Pekoe Fannings are small, flat broken leaf particles that occupy a position between dust and broken leaf grades. They brew a brisk, bright cup that is stronger than broken leaf grades yet more nuanced than fine dusts, delivering good color with moderate leaf character. Commonly specified in tea bag blends, retail loose packs, and institutional teas where a balance of speed and flavor is preferred.

Cup Profile

LiquorBright amber, good clarity
StrengthMedium to strong
AromaBrisk, fresh, lightly malty
InfusionQuick but not instant — balanced extraction
Common UseTea bags, loose black teas, export blends

BP – BROKEN PEKOE

Broken Pekoe is a coarser broken leaf grade that retains more of the leaf's natural structure and volatile aromatics compared to finer grades. It delivers a full-bodied, medium-strong cup with a pleasant briskness and noticeable fragrance on the pour. Preferred in markets where loose leaf character is valued — particularly for premium retail packs and leaf-dust blend formulations.

Cup Profile

LiquorDeep amber, full and bright
StrengthMedium to strong
AromaMalty, aromatic, slightly woody
InfusionModerate brew time, rich color development
Common UseLoose leaf blends, premium retail, leaf-dust mixes

BOP – BROKEN ORANGE PEKOE

BOP is among the most internationally recognized and traded CTC grades, prized for its consistent balance of strength, bright liquor, and distinct aroma. The broken leaf size allows for thorough extraction while preserving aromatic compounds that finer grades often lose. It is the benchmark grade for bulk export buyers seeking a versatile, high-performing tea that performs well both as a standalone and in blends.

Cup Profile

LiquorBright orange-red, vibrant and clear
StrengthMedium to full
AromaDistinctly malty, brisk, with a floral undertone
InfusionControlled extraction, excellent color and body yield
Common UsePremium export blends, specialty loose teas, bulk auctions

PEKOE

Pekoe is a whole or minimally broken leaf grade representing the more orthodox end of the CTC spectrum, where leaf integrity and liquor finesse take precedence over raw strength. It produces a lighter, clean amber cup with refined aroma and a smooth, lingering finish that appeals to discerning palates. Sought after for specialty retail, single-origin packs, and orthodox market buyers who prioritize cup elegance over intensity.

Cup Profile

LiquorLight golden-amber, clear and bright
StrengthLight to medium
AromaDelicate, floral, mildly malty
InfusionSlow, gentle extraction — best with 3–5 minute steep
Common UseSpecialty retail, single-origin packs, orthodox blends
