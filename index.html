Sourcing & Quality

At RK Natural Exports, every shipment begins at the source. We work directly with trusted farmers and curing units in Chikkamagaluru, ensuring full traceability from estate to export. Each lot is carefully monitored for moisture, grading, and defects, maintaining consistency that buyers can rely on.

To meet international standards, we also partner with accredited third-party labs for advanced testing, covering pesticide residues, heavy metals, and microbiological parameters. This ensures that what reaches you is not just authentic, but safe and globally compliant.

Because for us, quality isn’t a checkbox—it’s our promise.
