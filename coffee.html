RK Natural Exports sources and supplies a full range of Arabica and Robusta coffee grades from the Chikkamagaluru highlands of Karnataka, India — one of the country's most established fine-coffee growing regions. Our offering covers washed plantation grades, natural cherry-processed lots, and peaberry selections across both varieties, each graded and documented to international export standards.
