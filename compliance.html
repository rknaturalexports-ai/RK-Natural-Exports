Shipment Documentation Package

Phytosanitary Certificate

Issued by the Plant Quarantine Authority of India. Confirms the shipment is free from pests and plant diseases as required by the importing country's regulations.

Required by Destination

Fumigation Certificate

Issued post-fumigation treatment. Certifies the cargo has been treated to eliminate stored-grain insects and meets international biosecurity standards at origin.

Mandatory for Most Markets

Officially certifies goods originate from India — enabling buyers to claim preferential tariffs and duty concessions under applicable trade agreements.
